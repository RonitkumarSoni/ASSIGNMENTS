National News

Article 1:

New Education Policy Announced
Paragraph: The New Education Policy aims to make education more flexible and modern. It has replaced the 10+2 structure with a new 5+3+3+4 system. The focus is on developing critical thinking and understanding instead of rote learning. Vocational education, learning in the mother tongue, and the use of technology have been emphasized. The main goal is to make students globally competitive.


Article 2:

Floods in Assam
Paragraph: Floods in Assam cause widespread destruction every year. Heavy rains and overflowing rivers submerge villages, roads, and fields. Thousands of people lose their homes and are forced to live in relief camps. Crops, livestock, and property are damaged badly, affecting the state's economy. Floods in Assam remain a serious challenge and need long-term solutions.


Article 3:

New Metro Line Opens
Paragraph: A new metro line has recently opened, bringing faster and more convenient travel for people. It connects important parts of the city and reduces traffic on busy roads. The metro line is modern, safe, and eco-friendly. It will save time for daily commuters and improve public transport facilities. This new development marks a big step toward better urban infrastructure
